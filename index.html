Who are we?

Bio Constructions Group Ltd is a UK-registered company operating across multiple sectors, including:

  • Engineering, Structural and architectural consultancy

  • Digital education and e-learning services

  • Software development, with a focus on business and IoT applications

  • Wholesale and private label distribution of perfumes and cosmetic products

We work closely with trusted partners in the UK, Egypt, and the Gulf region to provide high-quality, innovative, and reliable services to our clients.

Our Projects

Bio Constructions, led by Eng. Amr Al-Baroudi, has 31+ years of experience and $5B+ in projects across key sectors.

The following are photographic glimpses of various projects he has led or engineered:

Strategic Maritime Project - Coastal City (Saudi Arabia)
Comprehensive engineering supervision of the implementation of construction facilities and infrastructure for a large port, as part of an integrated coastal development project across the country.
Integrated University Complex - Education City (Libya)
Design and implementation management of a university campus containing several educational and service buildings, with complete infrastructure in a desert environment.
Development of an Archaeological Area - Coastal Heritage City (Yemen)
Restoration and development of facilities and amenities within an area with a historical character, while preserving the architectural integrity of the surrounding environment.
Luxury Hotel Project - Marine Tourist Site
Construction of a five-star beachfront hotel with hospitality facilities, restaurants, and conference halls, under high-level engineering supervision.
